It's a simple application that lets you to use your green backdrop at home, and the best part is that you don't need any video editing software, either just modify the application by reassigning some variables or just play with the application as it is.
******************************************************************************************************************************************
Instructions:

1) Let the camera face a still background, and then press the 'c' key to capture the background.
2) Now adjust the trackbars as you need
3) Now just hide behind your cape and enjoy!
******************************************************************************************************************************************

PS: The application tries to avoid all the oranges by default
